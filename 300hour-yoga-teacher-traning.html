300|500 HOUR RYT TRACK BALI OPTION

300|500 Hour Yoga Teacher Training is ONGOING! Tymi Howard Yoga’s 300hour certification program in Vinyasa / Yin Yoga will qualify and fulfill requirements for the candidate to register with the Yoga Alliance at the 500 Level. Because of it’s “One on One” apprenticeship format, this program can be started and completed at the pace of the individual student.

300|500HR RYT TRACK BALI OPTION

THE YOGA BARN, Ubud BALI • JULY 2-23, 2019
Do you already have a 200 hour certificate and want to be certified at the 500 hour level? Then join Tymi for a three-week Yoga Teacher Training in Bali to get started on your 300 hours. The course in Bali will give you 200 hours towards the required 300 hours.

After the three weeks in Bali your remaining 100 hours will be covered through homework assignments, assigned reading, teaching classes, video conference calls with Tymi, and attending any Tymi Howard Yoga trainings, retreats and workshops held year-round at studios, conferences, and festivals around the world. 

Please remember: to exercise the 300-hour option, you must already have a 200 hour Yoga Alliance approved certification, which will then certify you for 500-hour level with Yoga Alliance.

      $4,800 financed: $500 deposit due at registration time
