Carlos Romero

founder of Livin' Inspired

Carlos Romero is a Surfyogi and a devoted Yoga teacher, who has been immersed in the journey of self-exploration through Yoga for over 15 years.
After finishing his first Yoga Teacher Training in Sivananda Divine Life Society School (2002) he has continued to deepen his Yoga practice and expand the scope of his studies in Asthanga, Vinyasa, Jivamukti, Dharma Mittra Yoga, Anusara, Yin and Embodied Flow. 
In 2005, he became a Thai Yoga Massage therapist and continued his exploration and studies into other forms of movement and healing. He discovered AcroYoga (2010) on a surf trip and since then has been dedicated to share this practice as a way to explore the connection and confidence to all those who feel the beauty of combining Yoga, Acrobatics and Healing Arts.

The Essence of Carlos’s teaching is to bring joy, inspiration and ancient wisdom from different arts into one, to experience yoga as a beautiful way to celebrate life and to embrace spirit which manifests within and all around.

Carlos spends most of his time in Bali, "The Island of Gods", sharing the path of Yoga & Healing Arts, allowing his true essence to shine in every moment. He brings people into their highest potential, getting them to move from their hearts, through sacred connection with nature, classes, workshops, immersions and retreats.
