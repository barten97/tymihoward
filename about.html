I AM TYMI HOWARD

International Yoga Teacher, Certified Holistic Health Coach and Owner of Guruv Yoga Studios in Orlando, Florida

I am dedicated to inspiring people to discover their life’s purpose through the art of yoga. 
My introduction to yoga began in NYC in 1993 as a professional ballet dancer with several nagging injuries and found my way into an Ashtanga class. 
Receiving my BA in Theatre and Dance from Rollins College and Masters Degree from NYU/ Circle in the Square, and too many Yoga Teacher Trainings to list, I continue to be influenced by so many gifted teachers.

Tymi is known in the yoga community as a “Teacher of Teachers” and a skillful graceful practitioner. 

Students fall in love with her dynamic, playful style of teaching, artistic sequencing and music! Self proclaimed travel junky, blogger and animal rights advocate, she is a lover of God, Life and Yoga. Tymi is forever a student and grateful to all of her teachers, students and family who continue to inspire!
