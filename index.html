Join Tymi Howard 500 E-RYT yoga teacher, host and presenter at international yoga festivals & events!

Practive with world renowned 500 E-RYT Certified Yoga Teacher, Tymi Howard. Her dynamic teaching style, wisdom and innovative teaching methods are the result of 25+ years of study, and over 20 years of teaching.  

Pioneering the Vinyasa Yoga movement in Central Florida

You can join Tymi Howard teaching and presenting at yoga studios and gatherings around the world; including The Yoga Journal Conferences, Wanderlust, Bali Spirit Fest, Bhakti/Shakti Fest, Sedona Yoga Festival, India Yoga Festival, Telluride Yoga Festival, Namaste AZ Fest, and on the regular teaching schedule at Guruv Yoga Studios in Central Florida and online classes at www.yogavibes.com

Tymi Howard
